About Us

The “Put the Mic on” musical talent show is about giving the contestants from anywhere in the country (Nigeria) a platform to showcase their talent in music with a maximum entertainment value. Furthermore, the talent show is targeted to host contestants within the age of 19-45 years Contestants will be screened online, to make easy for all participants to be auditioned without bias. Contestants will battle for the grand prize of 2 million Naira and the1st and 2nd runner ups five hundred thousand naira each with lots of other gifts. The 40 final contestants will win an all-expense trip to Abuja where they be logged in the talents hunt house. In addition, these contestants will be trained professionally in all aspects of stage managements and how to promote their art. Finally, the talent show is to run for 180 days, 90 days online screening exercise/battle, 45 days training of final contestants, 45 days of live show.
